StockViewer is an application that helps you visualize and analyze stocks, inspired by a bloomberg terminal. The central idea is to view stocks by sector and industries within each sector, visualize them along side each other and make informed decisions based on the data. The main screen will show you the list of all companies, the main chart, the comparisson chart, the metrics, etc. You can add stocks to your list and remove them. You can also create custom lists of stocks. The app also allows you to create your own portfolio and see how it performs in time.

It also tells you the key metrics of the company, like the p/e ratio, the dividend yield, the market cap, etc. in a very visually appealing way. It also tells you the metrics for the stock, like the price, the change, the percentage change, the volume, etc.

The application allows you to create your own lists and portfolios. The lists allow you to group stocks by sector and industries within each sector. The portfolios allow you to see how your stocks perform in time. You can also add stocks to your favorites list. These stocks will be shown in the "My Favorites" tab. 

Eventually, I would like to add the ability to see what the top analysts of a stock thinks of it. If the company is going to report earnings soon, what is the expected earnings per share, what is the actual earnings per share, and what is the difference.
